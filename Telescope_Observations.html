Telescope Observations


Always wanted to take cool photos of Galaxies, Nebulae, and Planets?
Wait no longer! We are bringing a series of events focussed on telescope observations, deep-sky and planetary imaging, and basic astronomical data processing! Due to the unpredictable Oxford weather, the events would be decided on short notice through a whats app group. The group also aims at co-ordinating astrophotography and astronomy activities around Oxford. So whether you are simply interested in observations or a seasoned astrophotographer join us now!
