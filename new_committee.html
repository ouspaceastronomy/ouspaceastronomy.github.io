Apply for new committee positions

We are expanding our team and we are opening up several places focusing on different aspects of Space and Astronomy! Take a look at the positions and their responsibilities below:

1. Telescope Officer - operate our telescopes during the stargazing and telescope observation events, take cool astrophotos, and join hands to restore the OUSAS Observatory (yes, the society has its own observatory!)
2. Space Liaison Officer - Coordinate with the space industry leaders around Oxford, and organize visits to space labs like the National Space Propulsion Test Facility (NSPTF) or European Satellite and Space Telecommunications Centre (ESA ECSAT)! (Great opportunity to network, if you are looking for jobs in the Space industry)
3. Events Officer - responsible for overseeing the planning of our events, organising astronomy outreach events at different Oxford colleges and liaising with other student societies.
4. Social Media Manager - increase the presence of OUSAS on various social media platforms, a digital way to popularise space and astronomy in Oxford!
5. Welfare Officer - to be a point of contact for issues relating to the well-being of society members, to foster positive experiences and relationships within the society.
6. Propose your own! - enthusiastic about a specific aspect of space and astronomy, but don’t see it included above? propose your own position.

Prior experience is not required but the love for space and astronomy is a must!
